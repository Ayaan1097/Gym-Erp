Weight Loss Plan

Ideal for BMI over 25

Our weight loss plan focuses on creating a caloric deficit through a combination of cardio exercises, strength training, and flexibility work. The program is designed to maximize fat loss while preserving muscle mass.

Weekly Workout Schedule
Day Morning Evening
Monday Cardio: 30 min jogging/walking Upper Body Strength: 30 min
Tuesday HIIT: 20 min interval training Rest
Wednesday Rest Lower Body Strength: 30 min
Thursday Cardio: 45 min cycling/elliptical Core Workout: 20 min
Friday Full Body Circuit: 40 min Rest
Saturday Active Recovery: 30 min walking Flexibility & Stretching: 20 min
Sunday Rest Day
Key Focus Areas
  • Caloric deficit of 500-750 calories per day
  • Combination of cardio and strength training
  • Progressive increase in intensity as fitness improves
  • Regular monitoring of weight and measurements
  • Complementary nutrition plan focused on protein-rich, lower-carb options
Expected Results
  • 0.5-1 kg weight loss per week
  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increased energy levels
  • Better sleep quality
  • Reduced BMI over time
